input:

first line: number of items (n)
second line: weights of items (n real values) 
third line: costs of items (n real values)
fourth line: knapsack's capacity

solution:

first line: number of selected items (value <= n)
second line: a binary array of length n: 1 - item was selected, 0 - item was not sected
third line: costs of selected items (n elements)
fourth line: total cost of selected items
